International Space Station (ISS)

The largest and most expensive Space Station ever built

20 years in low earth orbit(LEO)


Born from the collaboration of 5 space agencies for a common purpose

Its costruction began in 1998, and was completed in 2017. This year we celebrate the 20th anniversary of the ISS, which will remain in function until 2024. The ISS can host up to 6 crew members. the purpose of the station is to make it possible for astronauts to perfom experiment that on earth would not be possible. In addiction, it's possible to study the effects of a long stay in orbit by astronauts. The crew members are not always the same. This in fact changes periodically trough long expeditions.
